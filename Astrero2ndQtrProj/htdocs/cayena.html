Cayena Hill

The Villainess Is A Marionette

In her first life as Cayena, she's described to be a very wicked, spoiled, and jealous woman. Although she is very beautiful, it doesn't help with anything since she's very naive and gullible. She was eventually used by her younger brother as a marionette in his plans, and there she met her end. In her second life, she is a Korean who works under a ruly boss, she is often under-estimated and stepped upon by her superiors. She was stabbed by an unsuspecting passerby and there she met her end. In her third and current life, she vowed to get revenge and fix everything, and she's indeed currently slaying her third life.
