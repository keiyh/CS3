Welcome to the About Section of the site!

Unseen panels: treasured drawings; is a page or site wherein I upload panels of my favorite characters. The panels are those that I treasure so much. The reason? be it either because they looked beautiful and gorgeous in that photo, or the art was heavenly in that very specific panel.

But Unseen Panels is not limited to photos of characters only, this site also contains their: basic info, the things i like about them, and of course- my favorite panels of them.

The main inspiration as to why I created this page is because of my love for Cayena. I want people to get to know her since I love her very much and she deserves the world.

With the completion of this page, I wouldn't have been able to do it if it weren't for Abi and Hillary helping me while coding my home page, love u guys lol!

i hope you'll enjoy exploring this site, and get to know my favorite characters! Have fun! a compilation of profiles of manhwa characters I like.
