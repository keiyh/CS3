Most Beautiful Fictional Woman!!

Cayena Hill is a petite fair-skinned woman with back-length golden-blonde hair that is often styled in such a way that two of her locks are in the front. She has haughty icy blue eyes and wears various jeweled accessories along with luxurious and regal gowns having lots of laces and frills befitting of a royal. She is often seen with her hair loose, but she sometimes braids it with pretty ribbons whose colors match her outfit. She is the most beautiful woman in the Eldyme Empire that she is often praised for her beauty, and her beauty is such that it puts famous paintings to shame.
