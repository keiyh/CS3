Mob Psycho 100

Shigeo Kageyema, or "Mob" is a middle schooler that has amazing psychic abilities. Even though he tries his best to live a normal life, he always comes across major and catastrophic events, but he overcomes it with the help of his psychic abilities. But even with such esper abilities, the one Mob wanted the most was; to be close with a girl he likes in class. Will he able to accomplish what he truly wants?
