Cosette Parvis

  • Cosette Parvis is the older sister of the main character, Keira Parvis. Though they are related by blood, they do not get along well with each other.
  • She is a manipulative villainess that once k!lled her younger sister in their past lives. After getting rid of Keira, she went on a rampage and destroyed the empire.
  • She's the spitting image of Ludwig, that's why people doubt Keira is a real daughter. Appearance wise, she has golden blonde hair and red fiery eyes.
  • Even if Cosette is a villain, she's pretty anyways so she gets a pass.
