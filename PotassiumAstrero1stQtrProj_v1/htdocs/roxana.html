Roxana Agriche

Roxana: The Way To Protect The Female Lead's Older Brother

The protagonist of the story. In her previous life as a Korean, not much info was given about her except for the fact that she read a r19+ novel and now she reincarnated into the said novel. The original Roxana was described as someone who's unpredictable and has certain hobbies. She once ended up kidnapping Sylvia's (the Original Female Lead) older brother which led to her death once Sylvia got her revenge. The Roxana in the current timeline however, decided to help Cassis (Sylvia's older brother) so as to not end up in the same path as OG Roxana. In the process of helping Cassis, they developed feelings and he helped her in destroying Agriche- Roxana's household, as a form of revenge against her father for killing her older brother, Asil
